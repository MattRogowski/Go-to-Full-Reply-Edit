Name: Go to Full Reply/Edit
Description: Allows you to quickly go from quick reply to full reply or quick edit to full edit whilst saving any text you had previously entered.
Website: http://mattrogowski.co.uk
Author: MattRogowski
Authorsite: http://mattrogowski.co.uk
Version: 0.1.1
Compatibility: 1.6.x
Files: 2
Templates added: 2
Template changes: 2

Information:
This plugin allows you to go from the quick reply or quick edit to the the full reply or full edit page, whilst keeping any text or edits you had already typed.

To Install:
Upload ./inc/plugins/gotofull.php to ./inc/plugins/
Upload ./inc/languages/english/gotofull.lang.php to ./inc/languages/english/
Go to ACP > Plugins > Activate
